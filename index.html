The Holistic Group

The Holistic Group of companies is a conglomerate of enterprises managed by a team of professionals with an unflinching commitment to excellence.

The pioneers, Captain Ashok Kadian and Captain Jasbir Singh, commenced operations in 1995 in the domain of coal transportation. Under their able leadership, the business grew rapidly and today, the Group has diversified into logistics, BFSI, security and HR services. The Group has over a 1000 employees spread over five states in the India.

Holistic HR & Security Pvt Ltd is a wholly owned subsidiary of the Holistic Group. Headquartered in Gurgaon, it is in the business of providing Security, HR and Facility Management Solutions.

What we do

Holistic HR & Security Pvt Ltd:

  • Specializes in assessing current and future risks and developing & executing secure solutions to minimize their effect.
  • Offers end to end HR solutions with focus on executive search, recruitment, training and consulting.
  • Provides wide spectrum of quality facility management services.
